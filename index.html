Why travel alone, when you can have someone along. Our world have become so much virtual now. We are very connected to our friends but it's very rare that all of us could find some common time to go on a trip. This application will just help you do that. It will help you and your friends through an intuitive interface to plan a common trip.

You and your friends can collaboratively decide the places to visit, when to go and other details of trip. It will enable you to multicast your trip to a specific group which is better than pinging different friends every time to ask for his availability and miss out some friends who could have responded yes. You can also broadcast your trip plans and explore your friend's trip plans or someone stranger's trip plan (if it's brodcasted). You can join an existing trip by sending a request to broadcaster. Along the way you might end up making new friends and a more eventful trip.
